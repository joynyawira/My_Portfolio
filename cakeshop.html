Cake Shop

The Cake Shop website is a delightful online platform that allows customers to browse and order a wide variety of cakes for any occasion. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or just a treat for yourself, the Cake Shop provides an easy-to-use interface for cake lovers. Users can explore various cake designs, flavors, and sizes, making it simple to find the perfect cake.

Key technologies used in the development of this website include:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript for front-end development
  • PHP for server-side scripting
  • MySQL for database management

Features

  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
  • Variety of cakes available for different occasions
